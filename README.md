
# 🚀 Aplo Crypto Terminal

**Aplo Crypto Terminal** is a high-performance, minimalist web-based dashboard for real-time cryptocurrency tracking. Designed with a "terminal" aesthetic, it allows users to monitor market prices and manage their personal portfolio value directly from their browser or mobile device.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **Real-Time Data:** Powered by the Binance API for live price updates and market movements.
* **Personal Portfolio:** Input your holdings to see your total **Net Worth** calculated instantly.
* **Environment Presets:** Choose from 8 unique visual themes, including:
    * `Terminal` (Classic green-on-black)
    * `Midnight` (Deep blue modern look)
    * `Gold` (Luxury aesthetic)
    * `Retro` (Amber vintage feel)
* **Performance Tracking:** Automated snapshots to track your portfolio performance over 24h, 72h, 1w, 1m, and up to 6 months.
* **Multi-Currency & Multi-Language:** Toggle between **EUR** and **USD** instantly. UI available in English, Spanish, Russian, and Chinese.
* **Mobile Optimized:** "Pull-to-refresh" functionality and "Add to Home Screen" support for an app-like experience.

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1.  **Add Assets:** Click the **+** icon in the header to add new trading pairs (e.g., `BTCEUR`, `ETHEUR`).
2.  **Edit Holdings:** Click the **EDIT** button on the portfolio section to enter the amount of crypto you own.
3.  **Visual Analytics:** Use the **📊** button to view your asset distribution as a percentage of your total net worth.
4.  **Currency Switch:** Tap any price value to instantly toggle the entire terminal between USD and EUR.
5.  **Insights:** The bottom ticker provides interesting facts and history about the crypto space while you monitor prices.

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

* **Zero Backend:** This application has no database or server-side storage.
* **Local Storage:** Your holdings and API preferences are saved **locally on your device** (via `localStorage`). No one else, including the developer, can see your data.
* **Safe Code:** No external trackers or invasive scripts.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Stack

* **Frontend:** Pure HTML5, CSS3 (Variables & Animations), and Vanilla JavaScript.
* **API:** [Binance Public API](https://binance-docs.github.io/apidocs/spot/en/) (No API key required for public ticker data).
* **Persistence:** Browser LocalStorage API.
